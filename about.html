About Peter

Peter Stovall is a serial 0-to-1-to-100 product leader who has launched new products at seed-stage startups, driven market expansion at scale-ups, and built new business units within large enterprises.

Currently at Raptor Maps, Peter is at the forefront of AI-native product management, helping define new best practices where product leaders use AI prototyping for discovery, ship PRs and deploy agentic GTM workflows.

He led market expansion into critical infrastructure cybersecurity with the launch of Raptor Comply, a new compliance automation platform for renewable energy owners and operators, validating strong market demand before shipping a line of code.

Peter owned the product roadmap for core services - leveraging AI and robotics to accelerate the clean energy transition - that contributed to Raptor's successful Series C.

Previously, Peter pioneered product innovation in legal technology at two global firms, Fasken and Freshfields, establishing new product orgs and revenue streams.

As a product leader and a qualified lawyer, Peter embraces regulatory shifts as early product opportunities. He builds products that solve real operational problems and advance climate-critical missions.

Peter shares frameworks for launching climate-positive products in regulated industries on Medium and LinkedIn. He serves on the board of Climate Product Leaders and works as an ambassador for building products that don't cost the earth.
